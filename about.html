About Us

Our Story & Passion

We're more than just a pet shop – we're a family of passionate animal lovers dedicated to bringing joy through the companionship of exotic pets.

Who We Are

Chennai's Premier Exotic Pet Destination

Welcome to Chennai Pet's World, your trusted destination for exotic birds, dogs, and premium pet accessories in Kolathur, Chennai.

Our journey began with a simple passion – the love for animals. What started as a small endeavor has grown into Chennai's most trusted exotic pet shop, serving thousands of happy families across Kolathur, Royapettah, and surrounding areas.

We believe that every pet deserves a loving home, and every family deserves the joy that comes with a furry or feathered friend. That's why we carefully select and care for each animal, ensuring they're healthy, happy, and ready to become a part of your family.
